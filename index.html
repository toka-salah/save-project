what is safe ?

SAVE is a free Health education Informal peer led program that includes a combination of learning experiences designed to help individuals and community improve their health, by increasing their knowledge and skills and influencing their attitudes towards First aid procedures and Emergency Care. SAVE, this year, aims to broaden its goal and target public health with all its challenges. Our objective won't be only spreading awareness about 1st aid measures but also education of the society on means of prevention of different diseases of public concern.
